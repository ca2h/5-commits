Our story

The Great Grocery Cart Heist We went to the store for milk. Five minutes later, I was racing a shopping cart down aisle 7 yelling “HYPERDRIVE!” while you pretended not to know me. The cart hit a bump, launched chips into a stranger’s basket, and she just gave us a thumbs-up like it was perfectly normal. We left with glow sticks, a pineapple, and absolutely no milk — but a legendary story.
